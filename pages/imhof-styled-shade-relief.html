Imhof Styled Shade Relief

This project was a test to see if I could style a map similar to some of Eduard Imhof's maps. I used his maps as reference and followed some diagrams on his techniques for color layering found at reliefshading.com. I also found Carl Churchill's tutorial on making vintage shaded relief helpful for figuring out how to do the masking and adding details, such as the tree canopy. I am happy with how this test turned out after many failed attempts and will be using these techniques in future more intricate maps.

Tools: QGIS, GIMP, Inkscape
Data Sources: USGS 3DEP, GNIS, NLCD, OpenStreetMaps
