Mount Whitney Map

August 3, 2024


This project I utilized data from the USGS National Map for the elevation, hydrology, and point data. I processed the elevation data in QGIS, then added additional details and colored it in GIMP using DEM, slope, hillshade, and curvature raster. I was inspired by Tom Patterson's map of Going-to-the-Sun Road, Glacier National Park, Montana for the colors and tried to emulate that here. I also manually generalized the trails to for a cleaner look at this scale.

Skills: Cartography
Tools: QGIS, GIMP, Inkscape
Data Sources: USGS 3DP, GNIS, NDH, NLCD, OpenStreetMaps (OSM)
