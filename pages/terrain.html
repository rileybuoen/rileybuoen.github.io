Terrain Map: The San Gabriel Mountains

This was my final project for my Advanced Cartography class I took in University. It was a culmination of various techniques learned throughout the course from creating shaded relief, layer blending, and labeling. I took this map to the California Geographic Society conference in 2019 and won the 2nd place Student Paper Map Award. Looking back at this map I notice there is a lot I would do different, so that is why I am activly working on a revised version of this map with those changes.

Date: 2019
Programs: ArcGIS, Photoshop, Illustrator
Data: USGS, NLCD, GNIS
