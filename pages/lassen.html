Labeling Practice: Lassen Volcanic National Park

I undertook this project with the goal of reacquainting myself with labeling and working with vector datasets in ArcGIS and Inkscape. I used the National Park Service's Lassen Volcanic National Park map as a reference for label placement, color managment, and vector layers. I learned alot from the many nights of trial and error and I believe it gave me a good foundation for future cartographic projects I have planned.

Tools: ArcGIS, GIMP, Inkscape
Data: USGS 3DEP, NLCD, GNIS, OSM, NPS
