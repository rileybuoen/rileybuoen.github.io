Swiss Topo Mountain Style

For this project I wanted to try and emulate the style of swiss topo maps, specifically the way they depict rocks and scree in mountain terrains. I used the official geoportal of the Swiss Confederation as a reference for this map. The area depicted is in Alaska, because I wanted to be within the United States for this test.

I tried generating the rock detail myself, but couldnt get anywhere close to the reference. I then utilized the Piotr tool to generate them using a DEM. There is also a tool called Scree Painter I tried to use to generate the scree detail, but I couldn't get it to work properly so, using the stamp tool in GIMP I made my own and masked out the areas I didn't want scree to be depicted. Ill need to mess around with the application in the future to try to get it to work, but this worked for me.

Tools: QGIS, Piotr, GIMP
Data Sources: USGS
