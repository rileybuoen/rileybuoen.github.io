Crater Lake

This was a lab for Advanced Cartography at Humboldt State which focused on layer blending techniques and labeling. We used Daniel Huffman's tutorial Adding Shaded Relief in Photoshop to combine the hypsometric tint and the hillshade without darkening the hypsometric tint.

Tools: ArcGIS, Photoshop, Illustrator
Data: USGS 3DEP, NPS
