San Gabriel Mountains (WIP)

This is a work in process. I processed the DEM in QGIS, clipping to my project extent and preforming raster calculations for smoother elevation transitions in Blender. Blender was used to generate the hillshade with more realistic lighting and later further editing was done in GIMP to generalize and blend polygon layers into the terrain.

Tools: QGIS, Blender, GIMP
Data: USGS, National Forest Service, OSM
